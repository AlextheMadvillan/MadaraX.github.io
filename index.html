Who is Madara X?

Madara X, better known as Alexander Ferguson is an Entrepenuer with his focus in the realm of sound.
Growing up Alexander was trained in Classical piano for more than seven years, this was his first introduction to the world of sound and music. His love for the art only continued to grow. As he embarked on a journey for University to obtain his engineering degree
he once again became immersed in song-writing, singing, rapping, composing and later producing.
Four years and countless completed Audio engineering courses later, he has come out to re-shape the world of sound and music.
